CSCR

DHA Suffa University

The Career Service and Corporate Relations Department (CSCR) provides a range of services to undergraduate and graduate students in all areas of career guidance, counselling, internships and placements. The Center provides the resources, strategies that help students make positive career choices and develop their professional plans. The close linkages with the companies help to provide the best career prospects for our students.
